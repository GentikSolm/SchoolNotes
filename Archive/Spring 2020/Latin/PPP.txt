PPP:
    Shows gender

    The future active participle
    Forming the future active participle:
    Form the future active participle from the 4th part of a verb.
    Chop off the ”us” and add urus, a, um

    Translate the future active participle about to verb or going to verb

    Example:  amo, amare, amavi, amatus – love
    The 4th part is amatus.
    Chop off the us and you have amat
    Add urus, a, um and you have amaturus, a, um, which would mean about to love or going to love

    The future active participle declines like bonus, a ,um.
